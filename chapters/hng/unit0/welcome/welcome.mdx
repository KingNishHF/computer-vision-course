## Course Structure

Course ko multiple units mein divide kiya gaya hai, jismein hum fundamentals cover karenge aur state-of-the-art models ke bare mein detail mein jaanenge.

-   **Unit 1 - Fundamentals of Computer Vision**:  Iss unit mein hum computer vision ke basic concepts ke bare mein baat karenge -  jaise ki computer vision ki kya zaroorat hai, field ke basics, aur iske applications.  Hum image fundamentals, formation, aur pre-processing ke bare mein bhi jaanenge, sath hi sath feature extraction ke key aspects ke bare mein bhi.
-   **Unit 2 - Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)**:  Iss unit mein hum CNNs, unki general architecture, key concepts, aur common pre-trained models ke bare mein seekhenge. Hum yeh bhi seekhenge ki transfer learning aur fine-tuning ka use karke CNNs ko alag alag tasks ke liye kaise adapt kiya jaata hai.
-   **Unit 3 - Vision Transformers**: Iss unit mein hum transformer architecture ko computer vision ke context mein explore karenge aur yeh bhi jaanenge ki yeh CNNs se kaise alag hain. Hum common vision transformers jaise ki Swin, DETR, aur CVT ke bare mein seekhenge, sath hi sath transfer learning aur fine-tuning techniques ke bare mein bhi.
-   **Unit 4 - Multimodal Models**:  Iss unit mein hum text aur vision ke fusion ko samjhenge aur multimodal tasks jaise ki image-to-text aur text-to-image ke bare mein jaanenge.  Hum CLIP aur uske relatives (GroupViT, BLIPM, Owl-VIT) jaise models ke bare mein study karenge, aur multimodal tasks ke liye transfer learning techniques master karenge.
-   **Unit 5 - Generative Models**:  Iss unit mein hum generative models ke bare mein jaanenge, jismein GANs, VAEs, aur diffusion models shamil hain. Hum inke differences, applications in tasks such as text-to-image, image-to-image, and inpainting ke bare mein seekhenge.
-   **Unit 6 - Basic Computer Vision Tasks**:  Iss unit mein hum basic tasks jaise ki image classification, object detection, and segmentation aur inme use hone wale models (YOLO, SAM) ke bare mein baat karenge. Hum metrics and practical applications for these tasks ke bare mein bhi seekhenge.
-   **Unit 7 - Video and Video Processing**:  Iss unit mein hum videos ke characteristics, the role of video processing, and the challenges compared to image processing ke bare mein jaanenge.  Hum temporal continuity, motion estimation, and practical applications in video processing ke bare mein bhi seekhenge.
-   **Unit 8 - 3D Vision, Scene Rendering, and Reconstruction**:  Iss unit mein hum three-dimensional vision ki complexities ke bare mein jaanenge, aur Nerf and GQN for scene rendering and reconstruction jaise concepts explore karenge. Hum challenges and applications of 3D vision in computer vision ke bare mein bhi seekhenge, aur yeh bhi jaanenge ki yeh spatial information ka ek comprehensive view kaise provide karta hai.
-   **Unit 9 - Model Optimization**:  Iss unit mein hum model optimization ke important aspects ke bare mein jaanenge. Hum model compression, deployment considerations, and the usage of tools and frameworks jaise techniques cover karenge. Hum distillation, pruning, and TinyML for efficient model deployment jaise topics ke bare mein bhi baat karenge.
-   **Unit 10 - Synthetic Data Creation**:  Iss unit mein hum deep generative models ka use karke synthetic data creation ki importance ke bare mein jaanenge. Hum point clouds and diffusion models jaise methods explore karenge aur major synthetic datasets and their applications in computer vision ke bare mein bhi jaanenge.
-   **Unit 11 - Zero Shot Computer Vision**:  Iss unit mein hum zero-shot learning in computer vision ke bare mein jaanenge, jismein generalization, transfer learning, and its applications in tasks such as zero-shot recognition and image segmentation shamil hain.  Hum zero-shot learning and transfer learning across various computer vision domains ke relationship ke bare mein bhi explore karenge.
-   **Unit 12 - Ethics and Biases in Computer Vision**:  Iss unit mein hum ethical considerations specific to computer vision ke bare mein jaanenge.  Hum yeh explore karenge ki ethics kyun matter karte hain, AI models mein biases kaise aa sakte hain, and the types of biases prevalent in these domains ke bare mein bhi.  Hum bias evaluation and mitigation strategies ke bare mein bhi seekhenge, aur responsible development and deployment of AI technologies ke bare mein baat karenge.
-   **Unit 13 - Outlook and Emerging Trends**:  Iss unit mein hum current trends and emerging architectures ke bare mein jaanenge. Hum innovative approaches like Retentive Network, Hiera, Hyena, I-JEPA, and Retention Vision Models ke bare mein bhi jaanenge.
